Applying to Federal College of Animal Health and Production Technology

- Click on "Apply for Admission"

-Select Pogram (ND or HND), program type(Full-Time or Part-Time) and Course

-Sign up by filling the sign-up form and click on submit

Please note:

- ensure you use functioning phone number and email address that is
valid, accessible and functioning.

-After submission a verification mail will be sent to your mail

-Click the link in the verification mail.You will be redirected to the login page,
enter your email/jamb registration number and password.

-Pay the required Post-UTME fee of 2,000naira(for National diploma candidates) and
10,000naira(for Higher national diploma candidates)

-Complete the biodata form and upload all necessary documents

-Print out the photo cards and bring it along on the post UTME/screeningday.
This is what will be used to admit candidate into the examination.

-Continue to check your email for invitation for Post-UTME exam/screening.
